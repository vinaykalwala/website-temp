Payments policy


At Qtipstore, we aim to provide a seamless and secure payment experience for our customers. This Payments Policy outlines the accepted payment methods, security measures, and relevant details related to transactions on our platform. Please read this policy carefully before making a purchase.


Accepted Payment Methods

  • UPI Payments: Qtipstore accepts payments through UPI (Unified Payments Interface), providing you with a fast and convenient way to complete your transactions. Simply choose the UPI option during checkout and follow the instructions provided.


  • Credit/Debit Card Payments: We accept major credit and debit cards, allowing you to make secure payments using your preferred card. Our payment gateway ensures the confidentiality and security of your card information.


  • Cash on Delivery (COD): For your convenience, we offer Cash on Delivery as a payment option. You can pay in cash when your order is delivered to your specified address. Please note that COD may not be available for all products, and its availability will be indicated during the checkout process.


Payment Security

Secure Transactions: Qtipstore is committed to ensuring the security of your payment information. Our website employs industry-standard encryption protocols to protect your data during transmission.


PCI Compliance: Our payment processing complies with the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS), ensuring that your credit/debit card information is handled securely.


Order Confirmation and Verification

Order Confirmation: Once your payment is successfully processed, you will receive an order confirmation via email. This email will include details of your purchase and payment.


Verification Calls: In some cases, our customer support team may contact you to verify the authenticity of your order or to confirm payment details. This is a standard security measure to protect against fraudulent transactions.


Payment Issues


  1. Failed Transactions: If your payment is unsuccessful, please ensure that the information provided is accurate or contact your payment provider for assistance. In case of repeated issues, please reach out to our customer support at info@vindusenvironment.in 



  1. Refunds: Refunds for failed transactions or returned items will be processed under our Return and Refund Policy. Please refer to that policy for more details.
