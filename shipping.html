Shipping Policy


Order Confirmation: Upon successful payment, you will receive an order confirmation via email. This confirmation will include details of your purchase and a unique order number.


Order Processing Time: Orders are typically processed within [3 business days] of receiving payment. Please note that processing times may vary based on the specific product and its availability. The estimated processing time will be indicated during the checkout process.


Shipping Methods

Delivery Partners: We partner with reputable shipping carriers to ensure the timely and secure delivery of your orders.


Shipping Options: During the checkout process, you will have the option to choose from various shipping methods, each with its associated costs and delivery times. The available options will depend on your location and the products in your order.


Shipping Costs

Shipping Fees: Shipping fees are calculated based on the selected shipping method, the destination address, and the weight and dimensions of the products in your order. The total shipping cost will be displayed during the checkout process.


Delivery Time

Estimated Delivery Time: The estimated delivery time is calculated from the date of order processing and varies based on the selected shipping method. Please refer to the shipping options during checkout for estimated delivery times.


Delays: While we strive to ensure timely deliveries, unforeseen circumstances such as weather conditions, customs delays, or other external factors may affect delivery times. We appreciate your understanding in such cases.


Order Tracking

Tracking Information: Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with tracking information. You can use this information to track the status and location of your package.


Tracking Assistance: If you encounter any issues with tracking or have questions about your shipment, please contact our customer support at  info@vindusenvironment.in for assistance.


International Shipping

International Orders: We offer international shipping to select countries. Shipping costs and delivery times for international orders will be provided during the checkout process.
